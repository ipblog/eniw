IPGet Patent Search System Introduction

Rate this Content 3
     
4 Votes

 

IPGet Patent Search System is a patent search, patent downloading and patent management software for the patent worker, the intellectual property rights expert, the information expert, the legal expert and the scientific researchers. It integrates Europe, US , Japan, WIPO, Germany and so on ten authoritative free patent search websites. It can bulk download above websites patent's  bibliographic data, legal status, patent family , cited documents, abstract figure, patent first page, patent document. Moreover it provides the local patent search,  create categoris, customize fields, electronic files  management, patents share etc patent management functions, therefore it is also a simple patent management system.

IPGet Patent Search System has the personal edition, uses a access database, Suitable for individual users to manage a small number of patent data .. The IPGet Patent Search System also has the enterprise edition, uses a Sqlserver database, Suitable for business people to share processing or statistical analysis of large amounts of data.

IPGet patent search system is small and fast, all installation files total about 6.5M size . Simple and quick installation , ordinary users can install their own , completely without IT staff to help .

IPGet Patent Search System has follow major functions:

1. It has an embedded browser , integration follows the authority of  patent search websites,  it is a custom "Patent browser"

  1.  [EP]Espacenet - European Patent Office (not in free version)

  2.  [EP]EPO- Open Patent Services (OPS) (not in free version)

  3.  [WO]Patentscope - World Intellectual Property Organization (not in free version)

  4.  [US]PatFT - United States Patent and Trademark Office (not in free version)

  5.  [US]AppFT - United States Patent and Trademark Office (not in free version)

  6.  [JP]IPDL - Japan Patent Office

  7. [DE]DPMAregister - German Patent and Trademark Office

  8.  [DE]DEPATISnet - German Patent and Trademark Office

  9.  [CA]CPD - Canadian Intellectual Property Office

  10.  [CN]SIPO - State Intellectual Property Office

  11. [CN]PSS - State Intellectual Property Office (No pdf document, not in free version))

  12. [CN]CNIPR - Intellectual Property Publishing House (not in free version)

  13. [CN]IPWOM - IPWOM Navigation List

  14. [AU]AusPat - Australia Patent Office

  15. [KR]KIPRIS-EN - Korea Patent Office

  16. [TW]TPS - Taiwan  Patent Office

  17. [TW]TPS-EN - Taiwan  Patent Office English

  18. [HK]IPD - Hong Kong  Patent Office Chinese and English

  19. [IN]IPAIRS - India Patent Office

  20. [OTH] Patent Lens

  21. [OTH] Goolge Patents

  22. [CN]CPRS Patents Search.(not in free version)

  23. [CN]SooPat Patents Search.(not in free version)

  24. [CN]InnoJoy Patents Search.(not in free version)

When the search results show, click the download button , the search results will be added directly to IPGet 's downloading list.

Because IPGet patent search system directly integrated the patent search sites , so you can take full advantage of each search site's search function , as long as the pages can be downloaded from the site, then they can be downloaded from IPGet Patent Search System .  And the search results of IPGet  will be fully consistent with the results of the site.

2. Bulk high - speed patent download

Unlike download patents directly from the website, where can only download one patent  each time.

When download patents use IPGet Patent Search System, Each time we can download all the patents in one result page, and IPGet Patent Search System can auto turn the page, so it can bulk high - speed auto download all the search results.

From v2.0, IPGet Patent Search System Can also bulk input patent number to download.

3. Automatically merge the patent document

Unlike download patents directly from the website, where one patent has many independent pages.

When download patents use IPGet Patent Search System, it will automatically merge all one patent's  independent  pages to a single pdf document.

4. Selectable patent download items

When download patents use IPGet Patent Search System, patent download items can be flexibly configured , e.g we can only download patent bibliographic entries, or only download the first patent specification page, or only download the law status....

For example, for the patent statistical analysis, you can only download the patent bibliographic data, without having to download the patent drawings , patent full text and other time-consuming items, so that the patent download speed is quite fast .

5. Multi-level patents category management

IPGet Patent Search System can achieve a tree structure classified management of patents that have been downloaded , and users are free to copy or transfer patents between the various classification

6. High-speed stability download can be interrupted or restart or resumed at any time

IPGet Patent Search System can be set to download all the websites at the same time. For one website, it can be multi-task simultaneous downloaded .

We can stop the patents download at any time. and we can resume the download.

For the finished download patents, we can re-download again.

We can continue to the last time download. for example, last time, we download the result page 1~10 and stop for the network reason, when network restore ,we can download from the page 11, needn't download from the 1st page again.

7. Can easily viewed the patent's online information

We can view the patent's online information by click one button for the patents download use IPGet Patent Search System. So view the patent's latest information  is very convenient.

8. Local patents administration and search

The download patents saved in the local database, IPGet Patent Search System has add, modify, delete and other management functions.

We can do patent seach  by app no., title, applicant , inventor ... dozens of field. and we can do and/or search.

9. Custom fields function

IPGet Patent Search System has some custom fields for users, user can chang the field name, and can search the fields.

10. Electronic files management functions .

Patent documents downloaded from the website are saved in the patent's electronic files directory.

User can can also add other electronic documents into the IPGet Patent Search System to manage.

11.Statistics functions

IPGet Patent Search System can Statistics on the results of the query, and can export the result as a excel file.

12. Export to excel format

All the patents data items can be export to a excel files.

13. Export to ISI or RIS Format

IPGet Patent Search System's patents data items can be exported to ISI Web Of Science(*.ciw) format for sci2 similar Software to use, and can be exported to Reference Manager(*.ris) format for EndNote and similar Software to use.

14. Generate Patent Family Graph

IPGet Patent Search System's can generate patent family graph  by one click utilize the information downloaded from epo-ops.

15. Personal Edition and Enterprise Edition

IPGet Patent Search System Personal Edition use access database, Suitable for individual users to manage a small number of patent data .

IPGet Patent Search System Enterprise Edition use Sqlserver database, Suitable for business people to share processing or statistical analysis of large amounts of data .
